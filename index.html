Welcome to East Grinstead Scaffolding

Expert Scaffolding Erectors in Sussex

East Grinstead Scaffolding is one of the leading scaffolding erectors in the South East. We are based in West Sussex and weâ€™re in an ideal position to cover projects throughout Sussex, Kent and Surrey. We provide the complete range of domestic, industrial and commercial scaffolding services for customers in the area.

We built our reputation on our ability to provide the best quality scaffolding services in and around Sussex. We handpick each scaffolder on our team for their skills and hard work ethic. For this reason, we are able to focus on prompt and efficient service, while also adhering to strict health and safety standards.

Such efficiency reduces the time it takes to set up and dismantle your scaffolding, while also allowing us to offer our services at very competitive prices. We aim to provide a complete, end-to-end solution to your scaffolding needs. Whether your job is large or small, you can rely on us!

East Grinstead Scaffolding was established in 1993, by owner and Managing Director, Mark Daniels. Having previously worked as a foreman for major London scaffolding companies, Mark decided to go it alone, with a view to improving operating standards in the industry.

With this aim in mind, Mark set very high expectations as to how the company would be run. Mark decreed that his workforce would all be employed directly to ensure that every member of his team maintains his high standards; a rule that remains in place today.

All staff are CITB-trained and carry personal protective equipment (PPE). In addition, we carry full public liability insurance for your protection. We offer free estimates, a same day service and all work we undertake comes with an extensive guarantee. We offer fixed prices on all contracts.
