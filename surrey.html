Expert Domestic and Commercial Scaffolding in Surrey

At East Grinstead Scaffolding, our base sits on the borders of Surrey, Kent and Sussex. Weare therefore ideally situated to serve customers all over the wider Surrey area. We pride ourselves on providing excellent workmanship, as well as reliable and friendly customer service to all clients in the community.

Scaffolding Erectors in Surrey

All scaffolding erectors are trained according to the standards of the Construction Industry Training Board (CITB). Likewise, our experienced supervisors have the skills to delegate and to lead each scaffolder on the team.

Private and Commercial Scaffolding in Surrey

As each scaffolder at our firm is an experienced professional, we also have a wide skill base at our disposal. From small jobs on private houses, to large industrial excavations, we supply and install scaffolding with the same high level of expertise.

We work with both main contractors and subcontractors for a wide range of commercial scaffolding purposes. Our clients include housing developers, landlords, event organisers, film and television companies, airports, railways, as well as homeowners.

Health and Safety in Surrey

Health and safety lies at the heart of everything we do here at East Grinstead Scaffolding Services Ltd. With this in mind, you can be sure that all work is carried out in strict accordance with the Height Restrictions Act 2005.

We always carry out thorough examinations of the sites on which we are due to start work. This allows us to assess the specific needs for the site. This will detail the location and any restrictions that may affect work onsite.

This in turn allows us to determine the type and quantity of scaffolding that we require to undergo all work in the proper manner. It also ensures that we are prepared for any restrictions that may occur as a result of these aspects of the work.
