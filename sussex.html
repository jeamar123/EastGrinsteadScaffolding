Scaffolding Erectors and Suppliers in Sussex

East Grinstead is a highly-dedicated firm of scaffolding erectors in Sussex. We supply and install private and commercial scaffolding for clients across East and West Sussex and their surrounding areas.

The firm was founded in 1993, by Mark Daniels. Having worked as a scaffolder for a number of large firms in London, Mark noticed that both private and commercial scaffolding standards varied wildly from one firm to another. With this in mind, he decided to form East Grinstead Scaffolding Services to offer customers in Sussex consistently high workmanship, regardless of the size of the project. This ethos lies at the core of all work we do to this day.

How Do Our Standards Affect Our Scaffolding Work in and Around Sussex?

Health and safety is the most important consideration in all scaffolding work undertaken by the East Grinstead Scaffolding team. In fact, we consider that good workmanship and a focus on health and safety to be very much the same thing. We take this view that if a scaffolder is sloppy in one area of their work, then they are more likely to be just as careless in others.

Every Sussex-based member of staff is handpicked by Mark for their dedication to hard work and to doing a good job. Every supervisor on the team has a wealth of knowledge in scaffolding and in construction as a whole. They are also good organisers and are able to assign work properly and fairly.

As supervisors are responsible for the work on site, as well as for the safety of themselves and others, they must be responsible and able to act as leaders to ensure that work is completed to the highest standards, as well as on time and on budget.

Mark expects the same work ethic of his scaffolding erectors, each of whom is CITB-trained and very experienced in everything from small domestic projects to extensive commercial scaffolding work.

Such high standards are what keep our customers coming back. In fact, a large proportion of our work comes from personal recommendations and repeat business in and around the Sussex area.
